interests

My main undergraduate background is in symplectic geometry.

At the moment, I'm learning a little about (∞-)category theory, higher/derived geometry, and mathematical physics. I'm writing some of this down in a set of notes called Doodles of a Mouse (the title is a reference to Johnstone's Sketches of an Elephant). The hope—which grows more dubious every day—is that I will finish the Mouse by the time I start a PhD in the autumn.
